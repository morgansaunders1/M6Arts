ABOUT ME & MY MISSION

My name is Morgan Saunders, and I’m the artist behind M6 Arts. The name “M6” is inspired by my birthday, 6/6. Six has always been my favorite number and holds a special meaning for me.

I’m currently an art student at Arizona State University, but I grew up in a small rural town in Washington state. Creativity has always been a central part of my life, and I plan to grow M6 Arts beyond prints, exploring new mediums and ways to connect through art.

Alongside art, animals have always held a special place in my heart. I grew up surrounded by dogs and horses, and moving away for school made me realize just how much I missed that bond. This led me to start volunteering at shelters and fostering dogs, which opened my eyes to the many challenges animals face in Arizona and across the United States.

These experiences ignited a lifelong passion for animal welfare and a commitment to advocate for animals in need through every aspect of my life and work. My mission is to use my art to raise awareness, support rescue efforts, and inspire others to care for and protect animals everywhere.
